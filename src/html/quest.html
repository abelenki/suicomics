Similar to the Gallery, the Quest is also a game to make reading more enjoyable. While the galleries are controled by the readers, the quests belong to the books.

  • What is a quest?
    • A quest is a puzzle or exercise for readers to solve
    • It is usually made of a map with missing items
    • The goal is to find those items and complete the quest
    • The theme of a quest is based on the story in the book
  • What is it for?
    • Quests are created by the book authors
    • Some books can have multiple quests while others may have none
    • It can be a guide or hint of the stories in the book
    • It is designed to add more fun to the reading
    • And you are not required to complete the quests
    • It is usually impossible to complete it without reading the book
  • How does it work?
    • There is usually a background image showing a scene
    • There are a number of missing items in the scene
    • You can see the place holders and possibly hint where to find the items
    • The missing items may be scattered throughout the whole book
    • When all items are collected, the quest is complete
    • When complete, a reward will be given which can be a useful virtual goods
