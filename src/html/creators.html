Artists and writers, make money on Suinova Comics

How much free space can I use to store comic books?
Every author has a 5MB free storage space. If you comic page is a 10KB picture, you can store up to 512 pages free. Additional storage charges 100 Su Points (about £1.00 GBP) for one GB space per month. Space occupied by virtual goods is also counted.

  • What Suinova Comics do for creators?
    • We provide hosting of your own copyrighted works
    • We provide software tools to present your works
    • And monetize these works for you
  • Who can be our creators?
    • Every artistic reader can be a creator
    • Simply apply for a creator's account
    • And wait for approval
  • How to publish my work on Suinova Comics?
    • You create everything offline
    • Prepare one image file for each page
    • Then create a book on Suinova Comics website
    • And upload your image files page by page
    • You can publish while creating
  • What content/formats are allowed?
    • Comic books, Manga and Graphic novels
    • There's a list of genres you can select
    • And some content may not be allowed, please check terms of service
  • How to monetize my works?
    • You create items called virtual goods to sell to the readers
    • You set a price for your virtual goods
    • You can choose to display them in the public market or in book pages only
    • Readers will buy these goods using our virtual currency Su-Peas
    • And we pay the creators monthly based on an exchange rate, see terms for details
  • The Quests
    • Each book can have a number of quests/puzzles
    • It is a image of a scene with missing items
    • You design these items for readers to find through the book
    • Use these quests to engage your readers
  • Your Profile
    • You will have a profile on our website
    • And you can add your blog or other web links to it
    • You're encouraged to create a Facebook fan page
    • And keep your fans informed of your updates
