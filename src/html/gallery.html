Suinova Comics provides a gallery game for readers to collect virtual items from the books. Gallery Credits can be earned through daily visits, and used to expand the gallery space. One gallery room can be decorated for a particular story scene in a book or with mixed items from different stories. It is totally controled by the readers.

  • What's the gallery for?
    • Every reader has a free virtual gallery with limited space/rooms
    • The gallery is used to display collected items (decoration)
    • It is a show room to let your friends see your collections
  • What can be placed in the gallery?
    • Items to be placed in your gallery are collected from the books you read
    • They can be virtual goods you bought or given as rewards
    • Or exchanged with friends if possible
    • The items can be a flower, a weapon, a character, or a background picture
  • How to add items to the gallery?
    • Go to the Readers page (Readers button above)
    • Check your Inventory below the gallery space
    • Click Add button below the item you want to display
    • Wait and see the item sent to your gallery room
  • How do I manipulate the items?
    • You can move an item around in the same room
    • Resize an item (click it, drag the lower-right corner)
    • Put it above or below all others (click, up arrow for above, down for below)
    • Remove an item from the gallery and return it to the inventory
  • How to expand the space?
    • As you read more books and collect more items, your gallery is too crowded
    • You can expand your space by adding more gallery rooms
    • You can buy additional rooms or level up with Gallery Credits
    • Gallery credits are increased with daily visits
  • How to increase my Gallery Credits?
    • Login to your gallery every day to get a free credit
    • Send an invitation ticket to 5 friends daily
    • And you'll get a free credit if a friend visits your gallery
