Suinova Privacy Policy

  • The information we collect

    By installing Suinova Comics application on the SNS, you are authorizing Suinova to collect, store and use the following information:

    • Your user ID number
    • Your first and last name
    • Your profile picture or URL
    • Your email used to login to the SNS

    We also collect some technical information through Google Analytics for the purpose of analyzing the usage of our services in order to make proper improvement. Such information may include IP addresses, browser types, browser language, platform types, times spent on the pages.

    We require to collect more information about authors who submit their books to Suinova Comics portal. Such information includes real names, phone numbers, alternative email and address.

  • How we use such information

    Your ID, name and profile picture will be displayed on your own and your friends' web page when you use Suinova Comics to read, edit books, to play related games, and to communicate in the forums.

    We may also use your information for support, promotion of new titles, feedback collection, dispute resolution, and payment.

    We will provide your information to third party companies for payment processing, data analysis, delivery, hosting services. We do not provide your information to the advertisers or any other third party companies without your express consent.
