Suinova Comics is a social web portal for creators and fans to self-publish their comic books, manga and graphic novels and monetise by means of virtual goods.

How does it work?

On Suinova Comics, readers not only enjoy the books but also play the casual games. The games are associated with the book content.

Creators prepare both the book content and the virtual items to be used in the games. Some of these items are called virtual goods to be bought by the readers.

What's special about our service?

  • We provide comic books, manga and graphic novels to read online
  • There is no need to download a big PDF file or images
  • And there is no need to install any software on your computer
  • We do not ask our readers to pay before read
  • Instead, we need your support by buying our virtual goods
  • And some of these virtual goods are meaningful in the stories
  • As a reader, you will some games to play in addition to reading
  • Most books will have a couple of quests which make the book more interesting, see here for more
  • And you have a gallery of expandable space to display collected items and show off to friends, see here for more
  • If you are a reader, please read Readers Page for more information
  • If you are a creator, please read Creators Page
